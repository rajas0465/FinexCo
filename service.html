Our Services

Direct taxation

We provide expert Direct Tax Consultancy and Advisory Services to simplify the tax process for our clients. Our services include Tax Planning, computations, and negotiations to optimize tax incidence. We also advise clients on investments and compliance issues, and provide assistance in filing Income Tax and TDS Returns. Our goal is to ensure clients receive the best possible benefits while remaining compliant with the law.

Goods&Service Tax

We provide expert GST Registration Services to entities and individuals. Our services include assistance in filing monthly/quarterly GST Returns and obtaining GST Refund and Rebates. We also provide assistance in filing GST Annual Returns and periodic GST Tax Remittances. Our GST Advisory, consultancy, and other services are designed to simplify the GST process and ensure compliance with the law.

Accounting & Book Keeping

We provide expert Bookkeeping and Accounting Services to businesses of all sizes. Our services include maintenance of books of accounts, formulation of accounting systems and procedures, and periodic review of books of accounts to ensure compliance with relevant accounting standards. We also maintain various records and prepare monthly information systems (MIS) to keep you up-to-date on your financial position. Our goal is to simplify your accounting processes, so you can focus on running your business.

Inc.,& Registration

We provide expert Incorporation and Registration Services to help businesses get started on the right foot. Our services include drafting legal agreements, deeds, and other documents required under various statutes. We specialize in the registration of Partnership Firms, Companies, and Limited Liability Partnership firms under relevant acts. We also assist in the registration of businesses under the Shops and Commercial Establishments Act and help with Professional Tax Enrolment Registration. Our goal is to simplify the process of getting your business up and running while ensuring compliance with all legal requirements.

Documentary Services

We provide expert Secretarial Compliances, Reporting Services, and other Documentary Services to help businesses stay compliant with all legal requirements. Our services include compliance with Secretarial Standards and advisory services, as well as ROC Annual Return Compliances and Filings. We specialize in the preparation of financial statements and project reports, and can provide projected financial statements to help you plan for the future. Our goal is to simplify the compliance and reporting process, so you can focus on running your business.

Miscellaneous Services

We provide Other Services to help businesses with a range of administrative tasks. Our services include assistance in the application for Allotment of PAN, TAN, DIN & Digital Signature Certificates.
